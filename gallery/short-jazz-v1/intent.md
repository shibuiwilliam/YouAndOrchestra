# Intent: Short Jazz v1

Stylish, intelligent cool jazz for a midnight city bar. Tenor saxophone leads a smooth, unhurried melody over piano jazz voicings, walking contrabass, and brush drums. Tubular bell adds shimmering accents at phrase boundaries. Inspired by Kind of Blue-era cool jazz — sophisticated harmony with 7th/9th/13th extensions, nocturnal warmth, intimate atmosphere. 30 seconds, loopable.

## Emotion
- Sophisticated, cool, intellectually stimulating
- Nocturnal warmth — the feeling of settling into a dimly lit bar
- Suave and unhurried confidence

## Purpose
- Late-night jazz bar BGM
- Continuous ambient loop for an intimate city venue

## Listening Context
- Midnight bar in a city
- Dimly lit, intimate, low conversation in background
- Post-midnight crowd — regulars who come for the music

## Ensemble
- Tenor Saxophone — main melody, smooth legato, space between phrases
- Piano — jazz comping with extended voicings (min7, min9, dom7b9), harmonic color
- Contrabass — walking bass in quarter notes, chromatic approach tones, pizzicato
- Drums — brushes on snare, light ride cymbal, restrained and laid-back
- Tubular Bell — shimmering accent at phrase boundaries only (bar 1 and bar 9)

## References
- Cool jazz trio/quartet (relaxed swing, understated, spacious)
- Kind of Blue era — smooth, modal, unhurried
- Jazz club intimacy — close-mic'd warmth
- Extended harmony (min7, min9, dom13, ii-V-i, chromatic passing chords)
- Walking bass lines with chromatic approach notes

## Avoid
- Bebop frenzy or dense scalar runs
- Fusion energy or electric sounds
- Free jazz chaos or avant-garde dissonance
- Heavy swing feel (keep laid-back, almost straight)
- Loud brass, aggressive playing, or cymbal crashes
- Major-key brightness (this is Bb minor — nocturnal and warm)

## Key Decisions
- Bb minor chosen for its dark warmth — a classic jazz saxophone key
- 80 BPM for unhurried, contemplative pacing
- 10 bars (30 seconds) — concise loop for continuous ambiance
- Saxophone melody enters bar 3, giving piano space to set the mood
- Climax at bar 9 with bell accent, then texture thins for seamless loop
- Gradual arc — like eyes adjusting to the dim bar light
