# Intent: Puzzle Light

A playful, light-hearted 30-second loop for a puzzle game. Bright mallet instruments (glockenspiel, marimba) carry interlocking melodic patterns over gentle piano chords and soft drums. Minimalist, curious, unobtrusive — designed for repeated listening without fatigue. Inspired by Nintendo puzzle game aesthetics and Steve Reich-style mallet textures.

## Emotional Core
- Playful, curious, light-hearted
- Never tense or dark
- Maintains engagement without demanding attention

## Listening Context
- Puzzle game background music
- Repeated listening over extended play sessions
- Must not become annoying or fatiguing

## Sound World
- Toy-like, crystalline mallet timbres
- Bright, major-key harmony
- Minimalist interlocking patterns
- Soft, brushed percussion
