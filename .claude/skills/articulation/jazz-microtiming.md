# Jazz Microtiming Skill

## Swing Feel
- Swing ratio ~60% (long-short eighth note pairs)
- Not strict triplet feel — more relaxed than 66%

## Laid-Back Feel
- Beats 2 and 4: delayed 10-15ms behind the grid
- Creates the characteristic "behind the beat" jazz feel
- More pronounced at slower tempos

## Offbeat Delay
- Offbeat eighth notes: delayed 5-10ms
- Creates forward momentum without rushing

## Instrument-Specific
- Bass: Most laid-back (15-20ms behind)
- Drums: On the beat (reference)
- Piano/horn: Slightly behind (8-12ms)
- Melody: Most freedom (±20ms)

## Tempo Dependence
- Slow (<100 BPM): More laid-back (15-20ms)
- Medium (100-160 BPM): Moderate (10-15ms)
- Fast (>160 BPM): Less deviation (5-8ms)
