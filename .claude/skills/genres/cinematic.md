# Cinematic Music — Genre Skill

## Characteristics
- **Tempo:** 60-100 BPM (slow, majestic) or 120-160 BPM (action)
- **Keys:** D minor (epic), C minor (dark/dramatic), Bb major (heroic), F major (pastoral)
- **Time signatures:** 4/4 (standard), 6/8 (sweeping waltz feel), 3/4 (noble)

## Typical Instruments
| Role | Instruments |
|------|------------|
| Melody | strings_ensemble, french_horn, piano, violin |
| Harmony | strings_ensemble, choir_aahs, french_horn, brass_section |
| Bass | cello, contrabass, timpani |
| Pad | strings_ensemble, synth_pad_warm, choir_aahs |

## Chord Progressions
- **Epic:** i → VI → III → VII (e.g., Dm → Bb → F → C)
- **Emotional:** i → iv → VI → V (e.g., Cm → Fm → Ab → G)
- **Heroic:** I → V → vi → IV (e.g., Bb → F → Gm → Eb)
- **Tension build:** i → i → iv → V (rising 4ths, delayed resolution)
- **Deceptive cadence:** V → vi (subverted expectation for emotional impact)

## Section Structure
- **Establishing:** 4-8 bars, sparse texture (solo piano or strings), pp-mp
- **Build:** 8-16 bars, gradually adding instruments, mp-mf
- **Climax:** 4-8 bars, full orchestra, ff, dense texture
- **Resolution:** 4-8 bars, thinning texture, returning to opening mood, mp-pp

## Trajectory Patterns
- **Hero's Journey:** tension 0.2 → 0.5 → 0.9 → 0.3 (slow build, peak, resolution)
- **Suspense:** tension 0.4 → 0.6 → 0.8 → 0.95 → 0.2 (late peak, sudden release)
- **Pastoral:** tension 0.2 → 0.35 → 0.2 (gentle, low throughout)

## Avoid
- Rapid tempo changes (cinema needs steady pace for editing)
- Overly complex harmonies (clarity is paramount for emotional impact)
- Drum kit patterns (use timpani and orchestral percussion instead)
- Synth leads (breaks the orchestral illusion)
- Abrupt endings (cinematic music needs denouement)

## Reference Style Descriptors
Instead of naming specific composers, use these descriptors:
- "Wide open voicings, ascending motifs, major-minor ambiguity, meditative tempo"
- "Brass fanfares, dotted rhythms, strong cadences, heroic key"
- "Tremolo strings, chromatic bass descent, minor key, building dynamics"
- "Solo piano with sustained string pad, rubato feel, intimate"
